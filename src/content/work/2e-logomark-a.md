At one point, romina and I had been tasked to generate a system for tlon's ongoing product development efforts. I wanted to lean a bit on romina's dream-driven process and asked her once a season to meditate on our work and let her mind work out a symbolic representation of the product work and to draw it as a glyph. This system was the result.
